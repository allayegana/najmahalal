Our Story

Najma Halal Store is a trusted mini-market located in Seatac, Washington. We provide fresh, high-quality halal products to our community. Our goal is to ensure our customers have access to the best halal groceries.

More Than Just a Store

At Najma Halal Store, we believe in more than just providing groceries. We are dedicated to fostering a strong connection with our community. From offering culturally appropriate products to supporting local events, we strive to be a place where people feel welcome and valued.

We offer a wide range of products including fresh produce, meats, dairy, and specialty halal goods. Our knowledgeable staff is always available to help you find exactly what you need.
